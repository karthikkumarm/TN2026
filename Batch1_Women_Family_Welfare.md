## BATCH 1 SUMMARY

**Best fiscal value promise in this batch:** DMK's 1,000 industrial childcare centres (~₹800 Cr/yr; highest multiplier for women's labour force participation; only scheme rated ★★★★★ in this batch).

**Highest fiscal risk promise in this batch:** ADMK's Kulavilakku Scheme at ₹53,280 Cr/yr (26% of own tax revenue) — closely followed by TVK's Vettri Magalir Thittam at ₹41,100 Cr/yr (20%) and ADMK's free refrigerator at ₹35,000 Cr one-time (56% of capex budget).

**Recommendation:** Cap women's monthly cash transfers at ₹1,500/month targeted to SECC bottom-40% households (saves ~₹8,000–10,000 Cr/yr vs universal ₹2,000); reject ALL appliance freebies and festival consumption transfers; redirect fiscal savings to childcare infrastructure, SHG productivisation, and conditional education cash transfers (girl dropout prevention) — these have 3–5× higher economic multipliers per rupee spent.
