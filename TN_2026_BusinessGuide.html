🚢 Export from Tamil Nadu — Step-by-Step

Tamil Nadu is India's 3rd largest exporting state ($33.8B in FY24). Top exports: auto components, textiles/garments, electronics, marine products, leather goods, IT services. Here's how to start.

🌐 Key Export Bodies & Resources

🏢 DGFT (Directorate General of Foreign Trade)

IEC issuance, MEIS/RoDTEP benefits, advance/EPCG authorizations. Portal: dgft.gov.in

🚢 Chennai Customs / JNCH

Customs clearance, shipping bills, drawback claims. Online via ICEGATE

🤝 Export Promotion Councils

AEPC (Apparel), EPCH (Handicraft), MPEDA (Marine), CLE (Leather), EEPC (Engineering), APEDA (Agri). RCMC mandatory for benefits.

💰 ECGC (Export Credit Guarantee Corp)

Buyer credit insurance, political risk cover. Covers 85-90% of payment default risk. Essential for new markets.

🏦 EXIM Bank / RBI

Pre/post-shipment credit, buyer's credit, LoC support. RBI oversees FEMA compliance and forex regulations.

🌍 TN Guidance Bureau

State export facilitation, GIM connections, single-window for export infrastructure. investingintamilnadu.com
