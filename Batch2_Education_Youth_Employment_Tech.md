## BATCH 2 SUMMARY

**Most impactful employment/education promise in this batch:** DMK's Naan Mudhalvan scaling to 5L trainees (~₹900 Cr/yr; placement-linked with proven 60% conversion rate; highest employment multiplier per rupee spent in entire batch — only scheme that directly converts fiscal spend into verified jobs).

**Most redundant promise vs TN's existing baseline:** ADMK's NEET reservation increase from 7.5% to 10% — the 7.5% already exists (enacted 2022; SC-upheld); the incremental 2.5% affects only ~50–60 students/yr; does nothing to address the root cause (poor NEET coaching in govt schools). Also: TVK's unspecified college student stipend duplicates DMK's already-operational Pudhumai Penn without even specifying an amount.

**Strategic recommendation:** A fiscally responsible TN government should sequence education investment as: (1) Naan Mudhalvan skilling expansion + digital infra in colleges (highest ROI; ~₹1,100 Cr combined); (2) Breakfast scheme extension to Class 8 (~₹1,500 Cr; proven model); (3) Conditional student stipend at ₹1,500/month for bottom-60% only (~₹3,500 Cr); and REJECT all unemployment allowances (moral hazard) and loan waivers (credit freeze risk) — redirect those ₹18,000 Cr/yr into placement-linked training that actually creates employment rather than subsidising joblessness.
