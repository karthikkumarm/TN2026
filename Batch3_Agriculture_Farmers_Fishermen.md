## BATCH 3 SUMMARY

**Most fiscally dangerous farm promise and why:** TVK's full crop loan waiver at ₹1.5–2 lakh crore — this single promise equals 73–98% of TN's entire annual own tax revenue; would trigger a state credit rating downgrade; freeze bank lending to TN agriculture for 5+ years (RBI post-2008 evidence); and create irreversible moral hazard destroying farmer repayment culture. Second: ADMK's uninterrupted 3-phase farm power (₹15–20K Cr/yr; would accelerate both TANGEDCO insolvency AND groundwater depletion catastrophically).

**Highest structural value promise for TN farmers:** NTK's large-scale water body restoration (Ooranis; Eris) — addresses the existential groundwater crisis in 24 of 38 over-exploited districts; ₹1 spent on tank restoration yields ₹4–7 in avoided drought loss (World Bank); converges with MNREGS for cost efficiency; and has proven precedent via Kudimaramathu. Close second: 38-district cold storage network (₹500 Cr total for eliminating 15–20% post-harvest loss worth ₹15–20K Cr/yr).

**Recommendation:** A rational TN farm support framework should: (1) prioritise water body restoration + cold chain infra (highest ROI per rupee); (2) maintain MSP top-up at ₹3,500/qtl paddy BUT cap per-farmer procurement and pair with post-harvest loss reduction; (3) establish Fishermen's Welfare Board (institutional; self-sustaining via cess); (4) REJECT all loan waivers and 24×7 free power promises — instead, reform TANGEDCO via feeder separation (PM-KUSUM model) and convert farm loan distress into income-contingent restructuring rather than blanket waiver.
